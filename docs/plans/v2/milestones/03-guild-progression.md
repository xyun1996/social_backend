# 03 Guild Progression

- Status: `planned`
- Version: `v2`

## Goal

Turn the lightweight `v1` guild growth model into a richer progression and activity system without destabilizing the shipped governance baseline.

## Inputs

- `v1.0` guild baseline
- first activity template skeleton
- backlog themes for deeper guild progression

## Outputs

- deeper guild progression rules
- richer activity lifecycle and reward modeling
- clearer contribution and cap policies

## Acceptance Criteria

- guild progression is materially deeper than the `v1` skeleton
- activity systems remain bounded and operationally understandable

## Risks / Blockers

- guild systems can become feature-heavy quickly and overwhelm the social platform core

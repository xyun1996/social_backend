# Glossary

## account

The platform-level user identity that owns credentials and can map to one or more players.

## player

The game-facing role or character identity used by social, guild, chat, and party domains.

## session

A real-time authenticated connection context, scoped to a `player`.

## presence

Short-lived online state and contextual runtime metadata for a player.

## friend relationship

A mutually confirmed social connection between two players.

## block relationship

A directed relationship that suppresses allowed point-to-point interactions.

## guild

A long-lived organization of players with membership, roles, progression, and activities.

## invite

A time-bound request to join or connect across a domain such as friend, guild, or party.

## conversation

A message container for private or group communication semantics.

## channel

A message distribution surface such as world, guild, party, system, or custom channel.

## party

A temporary player group that supports coordination and queue entry.

## social queue

A queue state owned by the party domain that prepares a group for external matchmaking or related orchestration.
